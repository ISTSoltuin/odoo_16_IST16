Features

Property Details

Property Types

Manage all types of properties, including land, residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Keep separate details for each property like, address, longitude,latitude, landlords,website,licence no, construct year and more. also you can add parent property.

Residential Property

Manage residential properties and their details, including residence type, floor, number of towers, furnished/semi-furnished status, total and usable square feet, area measurements for bedrooms and halls, parking facilities, and more.

Land Property

Manage land properties and their details, name, used for, area in hector, land facilities, and more.

Commercial Property

Manage commercial properties with details like the commercial/shop name, type of commercial property, its purpose, facilities, floor, commercial shop measurement in square feet, and more

Industrial Property

Manage industrial properties with details such as the industry name, usage, location (inside or outside the city), facilities, asset measurement, and more.

For Sale / Lease

Manage Property For Sale and Property for Lease, set rent, sale price, and booking price respectively, and add extra tenancy services while creating tenancy contracts for leased properties.

Property Images / Floor Plan

Add as many images of your property as you want and define captions for each property image. You can also add floor plans of your properties for easy reference. keep all your property images and floor plans in one place

Amenities & Nearby Connectivity

Add amenities, property specifications, and various nearby connectivity options such as airports, highways, schools, malls, metro cities, metro stations, hospitals, and parks.

Documents & Certificates

Add documents such as brochures, utility certificates, bonds, safety certificates, license certificates, structural certificates, and more. Easily manage all type of important documents related to your properties in one convenient location.

Geographic Location

By inputting the longitude and latitude coordinates of your property, you can view it on a map, allowing you to get a better sense of its location and surroundings.

Property Maintenance

Create a property maintenance request and generate an invoice for the landlord to cover the maintenance charges.

Tenancy Contract

Tenancy Contract

Create tenancy contracts, you can customize payment terms such as monthly, quarterly, or full payments, as well as add additional tenancy services. You can also include broker commission with a fixed amount or a percentage of the rent amount.

Tenancy Invoices

The tenancy invoice is generated based on the payment term specified in the contract. If the payment term is monthly, the tenancy invoice is generated every month. If the payment term is quarterly, the invoice is generated every three months. In the case of full payment, all monthly tenancies are paid at the time of creating the contract. additionally extra service invoice generated along with monthly tenancy invoice if service is monthly.

Broker Bills

If a broker is involved in a tenancy contract, you can create a broker bill based on a fixed amount or a percentage of the rent amount. You also have the option to choose between a one-time brokerage or a monthly brokerage from the tenancy. The broker bill is created when the tenancy contract is active, making it easy to manage payments to brokers

Tenancy Contract Report & Tenancy Reminder

A tenancy contract agreement report can be generated, and customers can receive email notifications when the contract becomes active, as well as when monthly tenancy invoices are generated. This ensures that tenants are always kept up-to-date with their billing information.

Property Sold

Property Booking

When a property is on sale, the customer first creates a booking for the property using the booking price and their ask price, and then enters into the deal. Once the customer's booking is confirmed, a confirmation email is sent to the customer.

Property Selling

After selecting the customer and selling the property to them, after selling property a confirmation email is sent to the customer with information regarding document and confirmation of the property being sold.
